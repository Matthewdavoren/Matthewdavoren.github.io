About

As a Media Operations Engineer, I am dedicated to driving innovation and creating
unique experiences for audiences through the use of cutting-edge technology. With
many years of experience in the industry, I have developed a deep understanding of
the media landscape and have a proven track record of delivering successful projects.

My expertise spans a range of media technologies, including media asset management
systems, storage solutions, cloud based systems, transcoders, post-production software,
Linux, MacOS and Windows. I'm currently working on development projects by writing
Java, Javascript, SQL, HTML and CSS for front and back end development. Through
the use of these tools, I have become a strategic thinker who is always looking for
new ways to enhance user experiences and create engaging content.

Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to work with a diverse range of
clients across various industries including entertainment, animation, sports and
social media. I have also collaborated with talented teams of Software Engineers,
Developers, Designers, and Content Creators to bring innovative ideas to life.
